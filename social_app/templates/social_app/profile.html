How are the badge and levels calculated?

Level: Your level takes into account all the progress you have done on all of your workouts. It takes the total minutes you have worked out and converts that into a score.

Badges: There are 4 total badges you can obtain in this game, bronze, silver, gold, and diamond. Your badge also corresponds with your level, the higher the level, the higher the badge you will receive.
